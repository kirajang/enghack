Welcome to our Pomodoro timer

Use this timer to track your productivity.

The Pomodoro technique is a time management technique. The core idea is to focus on work for 25 minutes at a time, and take a 5 minute break in between. For each 25 minute session you record what you did, like a log. The 25 minute bursts help you stay focused, and over time the log sheet becomes a powerful tool for scheduling your time accurately.

Step 1: Enter number of sessions, and log your activity.

Step 2: Get to work!

Step 3: Take a break, re-evaluate, and start again
